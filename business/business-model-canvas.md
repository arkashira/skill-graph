## Customer Segments
* AI and machine learning developers, data scientists, and researchers seeking to manage complex AI skills and their dependencies
* DevOps and engineering teams requiring efficient collaboration and dependency management for AI projects
* Organizations with large AI and machine learning investments, such as tech companies, research institutions, and financial services firms
* Estimated customer base: 100,000+ developers and 10,000+ organizations globally
* Target market: Global, with a focus on the US, Europe, and Asia-Pacific regions, including Thailand

## Value Propositions
* Unique visual AI skill dependency management through a Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG)
* Improved collaboration and efficiency in AI project development and deployment
* Enhanced security and reduced errors through better dependency management
* Competitive pricing: $9.99/user/month (basic), $29.99/user/month (premium), approximately 349 THB/user/month and 1,049 THB/user/month respectively
* Free trial and community edition for open-source projects and small teams

## Channels
* Online marketing: social media, content marketing, search engine optimization (SEO), and targeted advertising
* Developer communities: GitHub, Stack Overflow, Reddit (r/MachineLearning, r/AI), and other online forums
* Partnerships: collaborations with AI and machine learning platforms, tools, and service providers
* Sales team: direct outreach to large enterprises and organizations
* Estimated customer acquisition cost: $100-$500 per user, approximately 3,500-17,500 THB per user

## Customer Relationships
* Personalized support: email, chat, and phone support for premium users
* Community support: forums, documentation, and community-driven support for basic and free users
* Regular updates and feedback mechanisms: ensuring customer needs are addressed and incorporated into the product roadmap
* Estimated customer retention rate: 80-90% annually
* Net Promoter Score (NPS) target: 30-50

## Revenue Streams
* Subscription-based model: monthly and annual plans for individuals and teams
* Enterprise licensing: customized plans for large organizations and enterprises
* Estimated average revenue per user (ARPU): $19.99/user/month, approximately 699 THB/user/month
* Projected annual revenue: $10 million USD (35 million THB) in the first year, growing to $50 million USD (175 million THB) by year three

## Key Resources
* Development team: 10-15 engineers, researchers, and developers with expertise in AI, machine learning, and software development
* Infrastructure: cloud-based services (AWS, Google Cloud, or Microsoft Azure) for hosting, storage, and scalability
* Marketing and sales team: 5-10 professionals with expertise in online marketing, sales, and business development
* Estimated monthly burn rate: $50,000-$100,000 USD, approximately 1.75-3.5 million THB

## Key Activities
* Product development: continuous improvement and expansion of the skill-graph platform
* Marketing and promotion: online marketing, content creation, and community engagement
* Sales and business development: establishing partnerships, pursuing enterprise sales, and expanding the customer base
* Estimated time-to-market for new features: 2-6 months
* Release cycle: bi-weekly or monthly updates with new features and improvements

## Key Partners
* AI and machine learning platforms: collaborations with providers like TensorFlow, PyTorch, or scikit-learn
* DevOps and engineering tools: integrations with tools like GitHub, Jenkins, or Docker
* Research institutions: partnerships with universities and research centers for advancing AI and machine learning technologies
* Estimated partnership revenue: 10-20% of total revenue

## Cost Structure
* Development and infrastructure costs: $200,000-$500,000 USD per year, approximately 7-17.5 million THB per year
* Marketing and sales expenses: $150,000-$300,000 USD per year, approximately 5.25-10.5 million THB per year
* Operational and administrative costs: $50,000-$100,000 USD per year, approximately 1.75-3.5 million THB per year
* Estimated total annual costs: $400,000-$900,000 USD, approximately 14-31.5 million THB
