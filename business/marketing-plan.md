# marketing-plan.md  

## 1️⃣ Positioning  

| # | One‑line Positioning |
|---|----------------------|
| **Primary** | **Skill‑Graph** – the visual AI‑skill dependency manager that lets engineers design, audit, and evolve complex LLM‑powered pipelines as a single, self‑documenting DAG. |
| **Alt 1** | Turn tangled LLM prompts and model calls into a clear, version‑controlled graph – so teams ship AI features faster and with zero‑surprise runtime bugs. |
| **Alt 2** | The “dependency‑graph for AI” that visualizes every prompt, tool, and model as a node, auto‑detects cycles, and guarantees reproducible AI‑skill builds. |
| **Alt 3** | From prompt‑library to production AI service – Skill‑Graph gives product teams a single source of truth for skill composition, impact analysis, and compliance. |
